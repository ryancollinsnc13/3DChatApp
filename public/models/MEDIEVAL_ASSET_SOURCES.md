# Medieval neighborhood asset sources

All exterior neighborhood models are downloaded assets licensed under CC0.

- **Fantasy Town Kit 2.0** by Kenney  
  https://kenney.nl/assets/fantasy-town-kit  
  Used for the fountain, trees, fences, market stalls, cart, and lanterns.

- **Nature Kit** by Kenney  
  https://kenney.nl/assets/nature-kit  
  Used for grass terrain and connected dirt-path tiles.

- **Retro Fantasy Kit** by Kenney  
  https://kenney.nl/assets/retro-fantasy-kit  
  Used for the town gate and guard towers.

- **LowPoly Medieval Village Pack** by Quaternius  
  https://opengameart.org/content/lowpoly-medieval-village-pack  
  Used for the finished placeable house and blacksmith models.

The original license files are retained inside each downloaded pack.
